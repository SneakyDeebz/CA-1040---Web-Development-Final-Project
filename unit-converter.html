Quick Reference Table

Handy conversions to keep in mind for class and everyday use.

Conversion Formula Example
Inches → Centimeters inches × 2.54 12 in = 30.48 cm
Centimeters → Inches cm ÷ 2.54 100 cm = 39.37 in
Pounds → Kilograms lbs × 0.4536 150 lbs = 68.04 kg
Kilograms → Pounds kg × 2.2046 70 kg = 154.32 lbs
Fahrenheit → Celsius (°F − 32) × 5/9 98.6°F = 37°C
Celsius → Fahrenheit (°C × 9/5) + 32 0°C = 32°F
Miles → Kilometers miles × 1.6093 5 mi = 8.05 km
Kilometers → Miles km × 0.6214 10 km = 6.21 mi
